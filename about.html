About me


I'm originally from Carlsbad, California, a small-ish city located in San Diego county. I grew up and lived there until I left for college. I went to school at the University of California, Rivereside (UCR) from 2012-2016.

After college, I started my professional career at Donnelley Financial Solutions in San Diego. At DFIN I was a Financial Services Account Specialist, kind of a blend of a project management/ customer service role. We were a primary point of contact for customers and oversaw the creation and filing of all types of securities related documents going to the SEC. I was lucky enough to spend about 2.5 years in that position and learned a lot about project management and managing expectations.

In July of 2019 I started work at Atlassian in San Francisco, CA as a web producer. In my time here I've had some very cool opportunities to create marketing pages for atlassian.com. Some highlights are creation of the marketing pages for Halp and Jira Work Management.
